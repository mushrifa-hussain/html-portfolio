The Best Movies According to Mushrifa Hussain

My top 3 movies of all time.

Demon Slayer

It is one of the best anime series. It is very iteresting and addictive. The animation is very good.

Qalb

This is a Malayalam Movie. This is one of the best movies ever. It is based on a love story and it has a very sad ending which will definitely make you cry.

Hridayam

Similar to the previous movie, this movie is also based on a love story. But this movie has a happy ending where both the girl and the boy move on and lead their own lives after the break up.
